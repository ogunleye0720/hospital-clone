Visiting Hours & Policy Hospital Amenities

Havana Specialist Hospital visiting hours are every day from 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. However, if you or your family member is in Intensive Care, you should check for restrictions due to condition, scheduled tests or therapy. In those cases, you can contact the specific unit for visitor instructions. Call 08027272727 for more information.
